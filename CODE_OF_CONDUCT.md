# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Please read the full text at the link above.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at conduct@renderbase.io.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
